About Us

A dynamic and fast growing sourcing company specialized in apparel fabric and sourcing, based in Sri Lanka, providing a vital link between customers & suppliers internationally and locally.

We work closely with manufacturing facilities of international companies, who are fully equipped with the most technologically advanced, efficient, and competitive facilities for all processes.

Our team has vast experience in the garment industry from product development to garment manufacturing and fabric dying to finishing. We have highly trained and skilled staff to provide the best customer support and after sales solutions. We are continuously on the look for new developments and trends in the market.

We are linked with many state-of-the-art apparel manufacturers in Sri Lanka / India / Bangladesh and China and undertake apparel orders for lingerie, casual wear, pants, shirts and many more items. We have very strong sourcing capabilities and our prices are extremely competitive.

The company has diversified its business line by exporting Sri Lankan spices, tea, coconut and coconut-based products to the International market.
